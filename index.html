Leshy Topology

LESHY is a brand aimed at enthusiasts of off-road driving.
The name of Lesha is the tutelary deity of forests in pagan Slavic mythology. Since the spirit rules the forest and hunting, it looks after both the equipment and the people who use the benefits of nature.

The Leshy system is inspired by military equipment, vehicles and functionality is at the fore. The whole system consists of off-road trailers, flat bad for vehicles and canopies that go well with the rest of Leshy's products. The production of all elements is made in Europe, in Poland.
All devices and components have the appropriate certificates and approval for use on European roads.

Professional Quality Work

Main assumption of Leshy was to design the trailer for the most demanding off-road conditions.

Solid construction and exceptionally durable materials ensure reliability and long life even in the most demanding situations.
